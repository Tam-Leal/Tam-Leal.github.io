Bike LED / wearable display

A 16×16 WS2812 backpack matrix driven by ESP32 and controlled from a React Native app over BLE. Live GPS metrics — speed, distance, and ride time — sync to the panel while pedaling; preset scenes cover SOS, turn signals, and visibility. A Studio editor composes JSON scenes that render identically on the app preview and the firmware.
